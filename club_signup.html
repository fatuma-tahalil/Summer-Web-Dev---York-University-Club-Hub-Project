Club Sign Ups

Join a York University club and find your community.

How It Works

  1. 1
    Choose a club

    Select the club you want to join from the dropdown or search on the Explore Clubs page first.

  2. 2
    Fill in your details

    Enter your student info and tell the club a little about why you'd like to join.

  3. 3
    Submit & wait for confirmation

    A club executive will review your application and reach out to your York email.

A valid @my.yorku.ca or @yorku.ca email is required to sign up.

Application Submitted!

Thanks for signing up. A club executive will contact you at your York email shortly.
