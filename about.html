About Club Finder

Built by York University students, for York University students.

What is Club Finder?

Club Finder is a student-built web platform designed to help the York University community discover, explore, and connect with campus clubs and organizations. Whether you're looking for academic associations, cultural groups, recreational teams, or volunteer opportunities, Club Finder brings everything into one easy-to-navigate hub.
